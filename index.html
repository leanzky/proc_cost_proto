Procurement Lifecycle

A comprehensive guide to the strategic phases of sourcing, acquiring, and managing goods and services to ensure organizational efficiency.

01

Requirement Identification

Defining the specific needs, technical specifications, and budget constraints for the required acquisition.

02

Sourcing & Solicitation

Identifying potential vendors and issuing RFPs (Request for Proposals) or RFQs (Request for Quotations).

03

Evaluation & Selection

Analyzing bids based on cost, quality, compliance, and vendor reputation to select the best partner.

04

Contracting & Award

Negotiating final terms and signing the legal contract to formalize the purchasing agreement.

05

Delivery & Performance

Receiving the goods/services and monitoring the vendor's performance against agreed KPIs.

06

Closing & Review

Final payment processing and post-mortem analysis to improve future procurement cycles.
